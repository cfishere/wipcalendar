Brief Overview:
The WIP Calendar contains open WIP Jobs (CASper jobs in order status "Work In Process").

There is in fact a separate calendar for each company (Outdoor Images, CSC, etc). The active company calendars are edited throughout the day by assigned admins. Once each day, before 8:30 am, a CASper report is pulled and updated to the calendar; any WIP jobs that are either (1) not already in the company calendar, or (2) in the calendar but with a conflicting date other than the new CASper report date, are imported. (Note: only existing calendar jobs marked as 'unassigned' will be removed and newly updated when a data conflict occurs).

Back-Up Schedule:

Each morning before 8:30 am two backups are created:

  1. "Yesterday" (A Back Up of Each Company Calendar from the EOD, previous day's admin edits)
  2. "Current" (A Back Up of Yesterday, including the newly-imported CASper data from that morning.)

FAQ: EMAILING THE CALENDAR

  1. Does Save Updates Send an Email Notification Out?
    ANSWER: No. It simply saves the current company's calendar to a permanently saved record.
  2. How Do I Email A Calendar?
    ANSWER: Browse to the MONTH You Wish to Email. Click the Button Labeled "Email".
  3. Who Receives the Emailed Month?
    ANSWER: HOVER Over the Button "Preview Recipients" to See the List. Note, You Can Click That Button to Copy the Entire List and Paste Into Outlook's TO: Field. This Way, You Can Delete or Add Emails to Edit the Recipeints if You Wish. (See: Attach PDF Month to Email).
  4. How Can I Attach a PDF Month to Email?
    ANSWER: View the Calendar's HELP Link, Which Describes the Process in Detail (above).
